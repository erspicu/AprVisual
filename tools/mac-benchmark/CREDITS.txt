AprVisual S1 — credits & data licensing
========================================

The AprVisual S1 engine is an independent C#/Rust reimplementation of a
switch-level (transistor-level) NES simulator. It reproduces the wire /
group-resolution algorithm of:

  * MetalNES  — transistor-level NES-001 simulator by Andrew Addis (iaddis)
                https://github.com/iaddis/metalnes
                (the S1 engine is a from-scratch C# rewrite of its wire_compute
                 core; no MetalNES source is bundled here.)

  * Visual6502 / chipsim — the original switch-level simulation algorithm
                https://github.com/trebonian/visual6502   (simulator code: MIT)

NETLIST DATA (data/system-def/*.js and the derived snapshot/*.aprsnap):
These describe the 2A03 (NES CPU) and 2C02 (NES PPU) at the transistor level
and are derived from the Visual 2A03 / Visual 2C02 projects by Quietust, which
are part of the Visual6502 effort.
