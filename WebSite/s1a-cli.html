The S1a differenceS1a 的差異What S1a adds over S1 — the always-on physics mechanismsS1a 比 S1 多的 —— 恆開的物理機制

Each S1a mechanism restores a slice of physics the binary switch-level model cannot express, and none of them touch S1's golden checksum (they arm only in test mode, never in the benchmark path). They simply run: every mechanism executes unconditionally in test mode — verified against the full regression (AC 141/141 · 147 146/1, Gate A golden 0x794A43ABDF169ADA unchanged). There is no per-mechanism toggle; the only escape is --no-shims, which drops the whole test-mode layer down to the pure switch-level engine. Two hand-written shims are also always-on, because no mechanism replaces them yet: OpenBus (last-transferred-byte replay — a behavioural ceiling) and DL (the $4016/$4017 input-data-latch φ2 transparency — deferred; its general form was unsound). See the shim ledger for the full RETIRED status.

每個 S1a 機制還原一片二值開關級模型表達不了的物理,而且都不動 S1 的金 checksum(只在 test 模式武裝,benchmark 路徑從不武裝)。它們就是直接跑:每個機制在 test 模式無條件執行 —— 並過了完整回歸(AC 141/141 · 147 146/1,Gate A 金 0x794A43ABDF169ADA 不變)。沒有逐機制開關;唯一逃生口是 --no-shims,把整層 test-mode 拿掉、降到純開關級引擎。兩個手寫 shim 也恆開,因為還沒有機制能取代它們:OpenBus(最後傳輸位元組重放 —— 行為層天花板)與 DL($4016/$4017 輸入資料閂鎖 φ2 透明 —— 延後;它的通則形式不健全)。完整 RETIRED 狀態見 shim 總帳。
