AprVisual

A Visual6502-style switch-level NES simulator & translation pipeline

Visual6502 風格的開關級 NES 模擬器與轉換管線

AprVisual turns transistor-level netlists of the NES CPU (2A03) into analyzable, verifiable, executable logic models — from raw silicon connectivity, through a logic/sequencing graph, to fast verifiable simulation backends.

AprVisual 把 NES CPU(2A03)的電晶體級網表 (netlist),轉成可分析、可驗證、 可執行的邏輯模型 —— 從原始的矽連線,經過邏輯與時序圖,到快速且可驗證的模擬後端。
