### 2. Collision Check for the Application

The exact formulation—using load-time structural physics to build static safety masks that gate discrete queue events—is highly plausible as original in the context of modern event-driven simulators. However, there are adjacent historical collisions you must distinguish yourself from:

*   **Classic Switch-Level (IRSIM / COSMOS):** IRSIM dynamically evaluates nodal capacitance to determine charge-sharing and memory state.
